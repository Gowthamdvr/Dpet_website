About US

Established in 1995

The Department of Computer Science at Tech University has been a beacon of academic excellence for over two decades. Starting with a modest intake of 30 students, we have grown into a vibrant community of over 500 students and 50 faculty members.

Our department is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and consistently ranked among the top schools for Computer Science in the country.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in Computer Science education and research, fostering innovation, ethical leadership, and entrepreneurship to serve society.

Our Mission

1. To provide high-quality education in Computer Science basics and emerging technologies.
2. To promote research and development activities in collaboration with industry.
3. To inculcate professional ethics and leadership qualities in students.

HOD's Message

Dr. Sarah Connor, Ph.D.

Head of Department

"Welcome to the Department of Computer Science. In an era where technology drives the world, our department stands as a pillar of learning and innovation. We believe in nurturing not just skilled engineers, but problem solvers who can tackle the challenges of tomorrow."

Our curriculum is continuously updated to align with industry standards. We encourage students to participate in hackathons, research projects, and open-source contributions. Our doors are always open for those who seek knowledge.
