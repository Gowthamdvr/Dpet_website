Academic Programs

B.Tech Computer Science

Our flagship undergraduate program providing a strong foundation in CS.

  • Intake: 120 Seats
  • Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
  • Focus: Programming, Algorithms, Systems

M.Tech Software Engg.

Advanced program for specialization in Software Architecture.

  • Intake: 30 Seats
  • Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)
  • Focus: DevOps, Cloud, Adv. SE

Ph.D. in CS

Doctoral program for aspirants willing to contribute to research.

  • Intake: Variable
  • Duration: 3-5 Years
  • Focus: Original Research, Publication

Course Curriculum (B.Tech)

Sem 1: Engg. Mathematics, Basic Electronics, C Programming, Communication Skills.
Sem 2: Data Structures, Digital Logic, Discrete Math, Environmental Science.

Sem 3: OOPs with Java, COA, Operating Systems, DBMS.
Sem 4: Design & Analysis of Algorithms, Computer Networks, Theory of Computation, Python.

Sem 5: Web Technology, Software Engineering, AI & ML, Elective I.
Sem 6: Compiler Design, Cloud Computing, Data Mining, Elective II.

Sem 7: Information Security, IoT, Major Project Phase I, Industrial Training.
Sem 8: Internship, Major Project Phase II, Seminar.
